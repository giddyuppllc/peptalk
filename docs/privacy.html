Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026 · PepTalk by Giddyupp LLC

What we collect

PepTalk collects only what you explicitly enter into the app, plus basic technical data needed to run it:

  • Account info: email, name, and profile details you provide at signup.
  • Health data you enter: mood, energy, sleep quality, meals, workouts, peptide doses, journal entries, body measurements, medical conditions, medications, and allergies.
  • Device health data (if you connect it): steps, heart rate, weight, HRV, VO2 max, sleep stages from Apple Health or Google Fit — only if you grant permission.
  • Crash logs: anonymized error reports via Sentry for stability.
  • Photos (optional): if you use Meal Scan, the photo is sent to our server for AI analysis and then discarded.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data.
  • We don't share your data with advertisers.
  • We don't scan your contacts, calls, SMS, or other apps.
  • We don't build behavioral profiles to sell.

AI features and data sharing

When you chat with Aimee, generate a recipe, create a meal plan, or scan a meal, the relevant content is sent to our AI provider (xAI / Grok) for processing. We strip personally identifying information before sending unless you've explicitly opted into personalization via the "AI data consent" toggle in your health profile.

AI responses are not medical advice. Aimee is instructed to redirect medical questions to licensed healthcare providers.

Where your data lives

Your data is stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS, US region). It is encrypted in transit and at rest. Row-level security ensures only you can access your own records.

Your rights

  • Export: Profile → Export My Data — download a JSON of everything.
  • Delete data: Profile → Delete My Data — removes all tracked data from this device.
  • Delete account: Profile → Delete Account — permanently removes your account and all associated records from our servers.
  • Revoke AI consent: Profile → Health Profile → AI data consent toggle.

Children's privacy

PepTalk is not for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

Subscription billing

PepTalk+ and PepTalk Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We never see your payment information. Cancel anytime in your device's subscription settings.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll notify you in-app. The "Last Updated" date above always reflects the current version.
